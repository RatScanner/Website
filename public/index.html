Rat Scanner

Open Source tool for Escape from Tarkov

Rat Scanner allows you to scan items and provides you with realtime data about them.

Items can be scanned instantly at any time!

Minimal UI

View all the data without distraction

  • Overlay it anywhere in your game
  • Adjust the background opacity to your liking
  • Make it show only the data you want to see

Quests & Hideout

  • Connect RatScanner to TarkovTracker.io via a API Token
  • RatScanner tells you what items to keep for quests and your hideout
  • See items needed by teammates after creating a live team on TarkovTracker.io

Installing

  • Extract the downloaded ZIP-Archive anywhere on your PC
  • Start Rat Scanner by launching  RatScanner.exe 
  • Once launched, go to the settings and set your resolution
